** AUDIO AND EXPERIMENTAL TYPE WAVES
Have optional limitations of audio friendly and full chaos wave construction

*** Audio Friendly
- This style will aim to anti-alias
- Ensure zero-crossing at start/end
- limit range of harmonics to fundamental x 8
- ideal for drag n' drop wavetable synths

*** Experimental Mode
- no anti-aliasing
- no zero-crossing correction
- up to 128 harmonics
- ideal for user with other library to control this
- ideal for user with llm guided use

*** normalize() function
- need to be able to take an =experimental= waveform and make it =audio friendly=
